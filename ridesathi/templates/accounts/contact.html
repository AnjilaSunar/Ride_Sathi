Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium bike rental services in Pokhara.

Do you operate outside Pokhara?
Currently, RideSathi only operates within Pokhara Valley (Lakeside). All bookings must start and end at our Lakeside station.
What documents are required?
You must provide a valid Driving License and either your Citizenship certificate or Passport as security.
Are helmets included?
Yes, one premium helmet is included with every rental for free. Second helmet is available for a small fee.
Can I book for a month?
Absolutely! We offers special discounted rates for long-term rentals (weekly/monthly). Please contact us directly for long-term deals.
Is fuel included in the price?
No, fuel is not included. We provide the bike with enough fuel to reach the nearest petrol pump. You must return it with the same level.
Is there a security deposit?
Yes, a small refundable security deposit or original document submission is required at the time of pickup.
